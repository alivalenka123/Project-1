About project

School Management system is a complete school management software designed to automate a
school's diverse operation from classes,exams to school events calender.. The school sotware has
a powerful online community to bring parents,searchers and students on a common interactive
platform.
It is a paperless office automation solution for today's modern schools. The school Management
System provides the facility to carry out all day to day activities of the school making them fast
easy,efficient and accurate..
